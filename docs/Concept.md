## Core Concept: **Resonance**

Instead of rotational stress/speed, your system could be built around **magical resonance** - vibrations in the arcane field. Two key properties to mirror Create's RPM and stress:

1. **Frequency** (like RPM) - how "fast" the magic pulses. Different machines require different frequency ranges to operate.
2. **Amplitude** (like stress capacity) - how much "power" the resonance carries. Machines consume amplitude.

## The Physical Component

Here's where it gets interesting - instead of shafts and gears, consider **crystal lattices**:

- **Resonance Crystals** - your "shafts." They conduct magical vibrations. Different crystal types have different frequency ranges and amplitude limits.
- **Harmonic Nodes** - like gearboxes. They can shift frequency up/down (trading amplitude for frequency or vice versa).
- **Tuning Forks** - placed on crystals to filter or amplify specific frequencies.

## Generation

Instead of waterwheels and windmills:

- **Ley Line Taps** - placed over naturally occurring ley lines (worldgen), generates steady low-frequency resonance
- **Moonstone Collectors** - generate resonance based on moon phase and sky visibility
- **Soul Condensers** - mob farms feed these, converts life essence into high-amplitude bursts
- **Ritual Circles** - player-activated, configurable frequency output

## What Makes It "Mechanical" Feeling

The magic here is that resonance **physically propagates** through connected crystals. You'd see:

- Visual vibration/shimmer effects along the crystal network
- Interference patterns when frequencies clash (two incompatible frequencies = destructive interference = power loss)
- Standing waves in closed loops (could be exploited or cause problems)
- Harmonics - certain frequency ratios create bonus effects

## Machines Could Include

- **Resonant Crushers** - high amplitude, low frequency
- **Arcane Synthesizers** - precise frequency required, crafts magical items
- **Teleposition Pads** - require matched frequencies on both ends
- **Golem Animators** - imbue golems with resonance patterns

## The Puzzle Element

Like Create, players would need to think about their networks:

- "I need 120 Hz for my synthesizer but my ley tap only gives 30 Hz... I need three harmonic doublers in series"
- "My amplitude is dropping too much over this distance, need higher-grade crystals"
- "These two branches are creating interference, I need to isolate them with a damping node"
